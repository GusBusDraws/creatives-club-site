About

Creatives Club started as a meetup for visual artists and writers in the r/WashingtonDC Discord server in August 2022. Eventually the meetups turned into a weekly club for folks interested in meeting regularly to work on personal projects and collaborations in the company of other creatives from all across the DMV. The group is now frequented by artists of all kinds, hobbyists and professionals alike. We see new members almost every week from folks that hear about the club from word of mouth or come across our Instagram page.
